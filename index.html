Getting started

What is Vinsaga?

Vinsaga is a maintenance log for your vehicles. Add every car you own, then record each service — oil changes, repairs, inspections, anything — with the date, mileage, cost, notes, and photos. Your whole service history lives in one place instead of scattered receipts and text threads with your mechanic.

Do I need an account to use Vinsaga?

No. Vinsaga works right away with no sign-up required. Your vehicles and service history are stored on your device.

How many vehicles can I add?

As many as you like. Each vehicle gets its own service history, and your Garage shows a summary across all of them — total services, total vehicles, and your most recent service date.

Logging services

How do I log a service?

Tap the add button, choose the vehicle, pick a service type, then fill in the date, mileage, and any notes. You can add photos before saving, or come back and add them later.

Can I edit or delete a service entry after saving it?

Yes. Open the entry from your service history and tap the edit icon to update any field, including photos.

Can I track cost per service?

Yes. Add a cost to any service entry and it'll appear alongside that entry in your history, so you can see what you've spent over time.

What if I don't know the exact date or mileage?

Date and mileage are the only two required fields — everything else, including notes, cost, and photos, is optional. Enter your best estimate; you can always correct it later.

Managing vehicles

How do I add a new vehicle?

From your Garage, tap add and start typing the make. Vinsaga suggests matching models as you type. You can also add a purchase date, mileage, VIN, and a photo.

Is the VIN required?

No, VIN is optional. It's there if you want a permanent record tied to the vehicle, but you can use Vinsaga fully without entering one.

What happens to a vehicle's history if I remove it?

Removing a vehicle removes its service history along with it. If you want to keep a record before removing a vehicle, we'd recommend exporting or backing up first.

Importing from CSV

Can I bring in service history I already have in a spreadsheet?

Yes. From a vehicle's page, choose Import CSV, then select your file. Your file should have a header row — date, mileage, and service type are the recommended columns.

What if my CSV columns aren't named exactly what Vinsaga expects?

After choosing your file, you'll get a Map Columns screen where you match each of your CSV's columns to Vinsaga's fields — date, mileage, service type, and notes. Date and mileage are required; everything else is optional.

Does Vinsaga handle different date formats?

Yes. The import screen auto-detects your date format and shows you a live preview (for example, converting "4/10/2024" to "Apr 10, 2024") so you can confirm it's reading your file correctly before importing.

Will importing create duplicate entries if I run it twice?

Each import adds new entries — it doesn't check against your existing history for duplicates. If you're unsure whether a file was already imported, double-check your service history before importing again.

Photos

How many photos can I attach to a service entry?

You can attach multiple photos to a single entry — swipe through them from the entry's detail page.

Can I take a photo directly in the app, or only choose from my library?

Both. When adding a photo, you can take a new one with your camera or choose an existing one from your photo library.

Where are my photos stored?

Photos you attach are stored with the app on your device, alongside the rest of your service history.

Troubleshooting

The app crashed or froze — what should I do?

First, make sure you're on the latest version of Vinsaga from the App Store, then restart the app. If the issue continues, email us with your device model and iOS version so we can look into it.

I imported a CSV and some rows are missing or wrong.

This is almost always a column mapping or date format issue. Try the import again and double-check the Map Columns screen, particularly the date format preview, before confirming.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email us at tylerstrongdev@gmail.com with a description of what happened, the steps to reproduce it if you can, and your device model and iOS version. Screenshots help a lot.
