Vinsaga for iOS

Privacy Policy

Vinsaga is built to keep your vehicle history yours. Here's exactly what the app does and doesn't do with your data.

Effective July 9, 2026
Before you publish this

This policy is written assuming Vinsaga stores everything locally on-device, with no account, cloud sync, analytics, or third-party SDKs — based on what's visible in the app today. If Vinsaga uses iCloud/CloudKit sync, any analytics or crash reporting tool, or sends data off-device in any way, those sections below need to be updated to reflect that accurately before this goes live. Apple checks that your privacy policy matches your actual data practices and your App Privacy "nutrition label," so accuracy here matters for review.

The short version

Vinsaga does not collect or transmit your personal data. Your vehicles, service history, notes, and photos are stored on your device. Nothing is uploaded, sold, or shared with third parties.

What we collect

Vinsaga does not collect, store on our servers, or transmit any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device to manage your vehicle and service records.

No personal data collected No analytics No advertising IDs No account required Vehicle data (on-device only) Photos (on-device only)

Information you enter

When you use Vinsaga, you may enter information such as vehicle make, model, VIN, mileage, service dates, costs, notes, and photos. This information is stored locally on your device to power the app's core features — building and displaying your service history. We do not have access to this information, and it is not sent to us or any third party.

Camera and photo library access

Vinsaga requests access to your camera and photo library so you can attach photos to service entries — for example, a picture of a completed repair or a receipt. Photos you choose or take are stored with the app on your device. We do not access, view, or transmit your photo library beyond the specific photos you choose to attach.

CSV import

If you import service history from a CSV file, that file is read and processed entirely on your device to populate your service log. The file itself is not uploaded to us or any third party.

Third-party services

Vinsaga does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services. There are no SDKs or frameworks embedded in the app that collect or transmit your data.

Data retention and deletion

Because your data is stored on your device and not on our servers, you're always in control of it. You can delete any vehicle or service entry directly in the app at any time. Uninstalling Vinsaga removes all app data from your device, in line with standard iOS app behavior.

Children's privacy

Vinsaga does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app does not target children and is intended for general audiences managing vehicle maintenance.

Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Material changes will not be applied retroactively without notice.
